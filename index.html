About

From DBA to AI Solutions Engineer.

I'm a Senior AI Solutions Engineer at Microsoft based in Bengaluru. Across 10+ years at Microsoft, Confluent, Oracle, and TCS, I've worked with 200+ enterprises across six continents — from Fortune 100 banks and telcos to public-sector and retail customers in EMEA, APAC, the Americas, Africa, and the Middle East.
